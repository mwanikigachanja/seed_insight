Average Yield: 12 bags/acre
Period to Maturity: 3 – 4 months
Special Attributes: Long stem and good root system, fairly tolerant to Striga, Rust, Leaf blight and Grey leaf spot, performs well in the moist mid altitude regions and the semi-arid lowlands, whitish in color.

Average Yield: 12 bags/acre
